About Us

Everyday more Aussies are going by renewable energy to cut bills, and create a healthier and better life for everyone.
Solar Citizens is an independent, community-based organisation that brings millions of solar owners and supporters together and keeps growing renewable energy in Australia.
We take action to protect the rights of Australia’s 2 and a half million solar homes and businesses for a fast and fair transition to clean, renewable energy.
